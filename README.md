  <h1>Privacy Policy — Olir & Olir Owner</h1>
  <p><strong>Last updated:</strong> 18 June 2026</p>

  <p>This Privacy Policy describes how the <strong>Olir</strong> (customer) and <strong>Olir Owner</strong>
  (salon management) mobile applications ("the Apps", "we", "us") collect, use, and protect your information.
  By using the Apps you agree to the practices described here.</p>

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Mobile phone number</strong> — used to create your account and sign you in via a one-time SMS verification code (OTP).</li>
    <li><strong>Profile details</strong> — name and, for salon owners, shop name, address, and service details you provide.</li>
    <li><strong>Location data</strong> — with your permission, your approximate or precise location is used to show nearby salons, enable "I'm On My Way" arrival alerts, and estimate travel time. Location is used only while you use these features and is not sold.</li>
    <li><strong>Booking information</strong> — appointments, selected services, and scheduling details.</li>
    <li><strong>Device notifications token</strong> — to deliver booking reminders and updates.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>2. How We Use Your Information</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>To authenticate you and secure your account.</li>
    <li>To enable salon discovery, booking, queue management, and arrival/ETA features.</li>
    <li>To send relevant notifications (booking confirmations, reminders, queue updates).</li>
    <li>To operate, maintain, and improve the Apps.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. How Information Is Stored</h2>
  <p>Data is stored securely using our backend provider, <strong>Supabase</strong>, and processed by our
  SMS verification provider for OTP delivery. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to
  protect your data against unauthorized access.</p>

  <h2>4. Sharing of Information</h2>
  <p>We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with service providers necessary to operate
  the Apps (e.g. backend hosting and SMS OTP delivery), and when required by law. When you book an appointment,
  relevant booking details are shared with the salon you book with.</p>

  <h2>5. Location Permissions</h2>
  <p>The Apps request location access solely to provide nearby-salon discovery and live arrival/ETA features.
  You may decline or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings; core booking features
  remain available without it.</p>

  <h2>6. Data Retention &amp; Deletion</h2>
  <p>We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and
  associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon request, we will delete your personal data within
  a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.</p>

  <h2>7. Children's Privacy</h2>
  <p>The Apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.</p>

  <h2>8. Changes to This Policy</h2>
  <p>We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.</p>
